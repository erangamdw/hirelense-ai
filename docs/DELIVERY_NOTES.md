## CV bullet options
Choose 2 to 4 depending on space and whether you want a product, backend, or AI emphasis.

- Built a full-stack AI interview and hiring assistant with Next.js, FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, Alembic, LangChain, and Chroma, supporting separate candidate and recruiter workflows.
- Designed a role-aware RAG pipeline that ingests CVs, job descriptions, project notes, and recruiter-side candidate materials, then generates grounded outputs with evidence references and saved report history.
- Implemented recruiter job, candidate, and shortlist workflows, including fit-summary generation, interview-pack generation, and same-job candidate comparison.
- Added structured candidate interview-prep flows for likely questions, answer guidance, STAR drafting, and skill-gap analysis, with citation-driven full-evidence viewing in the UI.

## Suggested portfolio summary
HireLens AI is a full-stack RAG application for interview preparation and recruiter screening. Candidates can upload documents and generate evidence-backed prep outputs, while recruiters can create jobs, upload candidate materials, generate fit summaries, and compare shortlisted candidates within a job scope.
